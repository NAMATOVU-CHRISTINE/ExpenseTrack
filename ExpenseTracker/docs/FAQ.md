# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is ExpenseTracker?
ExpenseTracker is a mobile app that helps you track expenses, manage budgets, and achieve your savings goals.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

### Does it work offline?
Yes. The app works completely offline since all data is stored on your device.

## Features

### How do I add an expense?
Tap the + button on the home screen, fill in the details, and tap "Add Expense".

### How do I set a budget?
Go to the Budgets tab, tap "Add", select a category, and set your limit.

### Can I export my data?
Yes. Go to Dashboard > Menu > Export to CSV to download your expenses.

### How do I change the currency?
Go to Dashboard > Menu > Currency and select your preferred currency.

## Troubleshooting

### The app is crashing
Try clearing the app cache or reinstalling the app.

### My data disappeared
Data is stored locally. If you uninstalled the app, the data is lost.
